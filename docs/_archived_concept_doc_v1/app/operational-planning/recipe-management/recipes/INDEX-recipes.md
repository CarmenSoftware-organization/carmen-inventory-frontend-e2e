## Overview

The **Recipes** module is the core of recipe management, providing comprehensive functionality for creating, costing, and managing recipes with multi-ingredient support, preparation instructions, and automatic cost calculations.

**Key Features**:
- Multi-ingredient recipe creation
- Automatic food cost calculation
- Yield and portion management
- Batch scaling support
- Preparation instructions with steps
- Allergen tracking
- Nutritional information
- Sub-recipe support (recipes within recipes)
- Version control and history
- Image and media attachments
