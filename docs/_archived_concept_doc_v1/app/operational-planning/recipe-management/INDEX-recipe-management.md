## Overview

The **Recipe Management** module provides comprehensive tools for creating, managing, and costing recipes in hospitality operations. It enables chefs and kitchen managers to standardize recipes, calculate accurate food costs, manage portion control, and ensure consistency across all food preparation.

**Key Capabilities**:
- ✅ **Recipe Creation**: Multi-ingredient recipes with yield calculations
- ✅ **Category Organization**: Hierarchical recipe categorization
- ✅ **Cuisine Classification**: Organize recipes by cuisine type
- ✅ **Cost Calculation**: Real-time ingredient costing and food cost percentage
- ✅ **Scaling**: Automatic portion and batch scaling
- ✅ **Equipment Tracking**: Required equipment per recipe
- ✅ **Unit Management**: Flexible measurement unit conversions
