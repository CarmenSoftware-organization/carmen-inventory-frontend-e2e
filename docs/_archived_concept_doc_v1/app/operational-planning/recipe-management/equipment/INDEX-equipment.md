## Overview

The **Kitchen Equipment** module manages equipment definitions that can be associated with recipes. This helps kitchen staff understand equipment requirements for recipe preparation and supports capacity planning.

**Key Features**:
- Equipment catalog management
- Equipment type classification
- Recipe equipment assignments
- Capacity and specifications
- Maintenance tracking integration
- Active/inactive status
